WHY TWELV

Economic uncertainty is a dark, ominous cloud that looms over the world population. Even here in the United States, the most economically gifted country in the world, 77% of the population feels anxious and worried about their financial situation. The worries include a broad range of issues, from savings and retirement, to affording mortgage payments and providing higher education for their children.

On top of all of this financial uncertainty, we are failing mother earth. Climates are changing, deforestation accelerates, and we have ceased to be stewards of our oceans.

Who We Are

Twelv is an innovative platform that directly addresses the two biggest problems we all face;
a lack of financial opportunity, and how to support a planet that needs healing.
