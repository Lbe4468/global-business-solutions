WHY TWELV

Economic uncertainty is a dark, ominous cloud that looms over the world population. Even here in the United States, the most economically gifted country in the world, 77% of the population feels anxious and worried about their financial situation. The worries include a broad range of issues, from savings and retirement, to affording mortgage payments and providing higher education for their children.

On top of all of this financial uncertainty, we are failing mother earth. Climates are changing, deforestation accelerates, and we have ceased to be stewards of our oceans.

Who We Are

Twelv is an innovative platform that directly addresses the two biggest problems we all face;
a lack of financial opportunity, and how to support a planet that needs healing.

Twelv resolves these issues by providing access to a revolutionary, global financial system that includes the services listed below.

Lowest Carbon Denominator (LCD)

Through a strategic alliance with the EEX, Twelv is able to offer the LCD Staking Program which provides a unique opportunity for users to actively support environmental initiatives. By minting and owning these NFTs, users contribute to sustainability efforts and raise awareness about the importance of eco-conscious practices. What makes the program special is that LCD NFTs also provide users with daily rewards (LCD Tokens) that can be swapped for digital currencies such as USDT or USDC.

Global Banking

Twelv is able to provide basic banking services that span the world, aiming to service the billions of un-banked and under-served. Twelv stands as a symbol of accessibility, offering the financial services necessary to meet the needs of our members.

The Twelv Account

By leveraging its valuable partnerships, Twelv is able to give its members access to a unique, high-interest, hybrid savings account. The Twelv Account generates its members 12% APY, effectively setting them on the path to long-term financial well-being.

Financial Opportunity

Further bolstering the aforementioned services, Twelv has created a unique referral plan that allows its members to generate additional income and meet their financial requirements. We also provide the education and tools necessary for our members to properly manage their economy, and ultimately live debt-free.
